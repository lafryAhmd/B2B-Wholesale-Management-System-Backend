How It Works

Trade stock in three simple steps

From sign-up to your first deal — StockBridge is built for speed, simplicity, and secure B2B commerce.

01

Register & Verify

Create your business account in minutes. Our team verifies your registration number to ensure all buyers and sellers are legitimate B2B entities.

02

List or Browse

Post your surplus inventory with price, quantity, and photos — or browse thousands of live listings filtered by industry, location, and price range.

03

Deal & Get Paid

Negotiate, agree terms, and complete transactions securely on-platform. Funds are protected until both parties confirm delivery and acceptance.

Features

Everything you need to trade smarter

Built specifically for the demands of B2B stock trading — powerful, fast, and secure.

Live Inventory Feed

Real-time listings updated as businesses post and close deals. Set alerts for categories you're buying in and never miss a bargain.

Verified Business Network

Every member is KYB-verified. No individual consumers — just trusted wholesale buyers and sellers, reducing fraud risk dramatically.

Map-Based Discovery

Find buyers and sellers near you using our integrated Google Maps search. Reduce logistics costs by sourcing locally.

Smart Dashboard

Track your listings, deals in progress, revenue, and network insights from a single clean dashboard designed for busy traders.
